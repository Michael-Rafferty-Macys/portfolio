Introduction

About Me

I live in Huntsville, Alabama: the Rocket City. Why is Huntsville called the Rocket city? There are several reasons:

  • Dr. Wernher von Braun and his team set up their development facilities in Huntsville, Alabama.
  • Huntsville, Alabama is where rockets were developed that put men on the moon.
  • Huntsville, Alabama is the home of the U.S. Space & Rocket Center; the largest space museum in the world.
